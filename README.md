# Task-2-Data-Visualization-and-Storytelling
Superstore Sales Dashboard – Tableau Public Story

A simple and clear data story using Sample Superstore data.

What you will see:
1. Total Sales – Overall $2.3 million in sales
2. Yearly Total Sales – How sales grew every year
3. Yearly Region-wise Sales – Which regions sell the most
4. Executive Summary – Total sales, profit, and profit ratio in big numbers
5. Profit Map – Red states = losing money, Green states = making money

Key Insight: We sell a lot, but Furniture + high discounts + some states are killing our profit.
